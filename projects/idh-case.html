IDH - User research & website improvement

Background

IDH - The sustainable trade initiative, is based in Amsterdam and aims to improve the economic, social and environmental sustainability of production systems in developing countries, focusing on internationally traded commodities.

I am currently working at IDH as freelance UX researcher. We are conducting a series of analysis on the website and their users in order to improve the current website. My tasks include website heuristic analysis, conduct and analyse interviews of IDH's stakeholders and IDH's users, conduct and analyse usability testings, create design documents such as personas and customer journeys and finally compile the user research output to make recommendations for the website's improvements. The project is still on-going, I can share more information upon request.

IDH is a great company, working towards sustainability. You can read more about them here.
