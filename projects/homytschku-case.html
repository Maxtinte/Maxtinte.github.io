Homytschku - Sustainable real estate

Background

The goal of this UI exercise was to design a responsive web app that provides private real estate investors with information on properties of interest.

This is a twist on existing real estate platforms in that the website focuses on green buildings that are energy efficient, source sustainable construction material and respect their environment.

For this project, I was provided with a brief including a persona and some user stories.
