Homytschku - Sustainable real estate

Background

The goal of this UI exercise was to design a responsive web app that provides private real estate investors with information on properties of interest.

For this project, I was provided with a brief including a persona and some user stories. I was tasked to handle the research, wireframing, design and prototyping of the website.

How did I get there?

After a quick market research and competitive analysis, I developed a user flow diagram to break down the possible routes a user could take in the web app. This allowed me to develop the wireframes and to come up with the functionalities. Some of the functionalities that were added worth mentioning are a smart filtering system, a 360 video tour and on-site information in real-time via geolocalisation.

Additionally I noticed that sustainability criteria in housing are often ignored, so green labels highlighting environmental and societal topics such as water management and energy efficiency were added to the properties information.
