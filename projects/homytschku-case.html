Homytschku - Sustainable real estate

Background

While there are plenty of blogs and agencies providing information on real estate investment, often, sustainability criteria in housing are ignored.
The purpose of this website is to help investors invest in green buildings that are energy efficient, source sustainable construction material and respect their environment.
This is a twist on existing real estate platforms in that the website is designed for those unfamiliar with real estate investment. It allows investors to search through properties of interest and provides them with recommendations based on their investment criteria.
